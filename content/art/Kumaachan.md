### What is it about?

This is a 2D vtuber avatar that I created for my streamer-friend [@achan_jp](https://x.com/achanjp19). It was created with the use of Painttool Sai and Live2D in the span of roughly one week - I made best use of the time before Live2D trial runs out. I found the software pretty understandable and fine to work with, especially with all the tutorials and workflow VODs available, though there was a certain learning curve to it. I am ready to further learn rigging of 2D animated characters once I will have a need for it in any new projects I work on.
