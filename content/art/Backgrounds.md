### What is it about?

I created a series of painterly backgrounds for use in games. Most of these backgrounds have customizable features like daytime and changing environment details. I am successfully selling some of these assets of platforms like itch.io and GameDev Market. I also drew and used backgrounds in this artstyle in my visual novel "What to do with the four-eared fox". And I used similar approach when designing some player banners for Geotastic. 

The backgrounds were drawn with pencil on paper, and then scanned in, cleaned up and colored digitally.
