In January 2024, I created a custom PNG lava lamp asset for use in the livestreams of Neuro-sama, an AI vtuber developed by the Twitch streamer vedal987. In May 2024, I also designed a plasma globe asset for his second character Evil Neuro.

::lightbox-images{:items='[ "lamps.png" ]'}
::

These assets have been consistently used in a high-visibility livestream environment and contributed to the visual identity of the stream. The lava lamp design later served as the basis for physical merchandise.
